## Positioning
1-line: Solidity Sentry — Your Ultimate Solidity Code Analysis and Optimization Tool

Alternatives:
1. Solidity Savvy — Code Quality for Solidity Developers
2. SolidCodeGuard — Enhance Solidity Code Efficiency and Security
3. Solidity Scout — Your Trusted Partner for Solidity Code Analysis

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Alex Johnson, Senior Solidity Developer at Blockchain Inc.
- Daily Job: Leads a team of Solidity developers, responsible for building and maintaining smart contracts for various blockchain projects
- Budget: $10,000+ per year for tools and services to improve code quality and efficiency

### Persona 2: Sarah Lee, Freelance Solidity Developer
- Daily Job: Works on a variety of blockchain projects as a freelance Solidity developer, focusing on optimizing code for performance and security
- Budget: $5,000+ per year for tools and services that help her deliver high-quality work to clients

### Persona 3: James Kim, Blockchain Educator and Consultant
- Daily Job: Teaches blockchain development and provides consulting services to businesses and individuals interested in blockchain technology
- Budget: $2,000+ per year for tools and resources to stay updated on the latest trends and best practices in Solidity development

## Channels
1. Direct Outreach (CAC estimate: $500 per customer)
   - Targeted email campaigns to Solidity developers and blockchain companies
   - Personalized outreach to freelancers and educators through LinkedIn and other professional networks

2. Content Marketing (CAC estimate: $200 per customer)
   - Blog posts, tutorials, and webinars on Solidity development best practices and Solidity Sentry features
   - Guest posts on popular blockchain and Solidity development blogs

3. Partnerships (CAC estimate: $100 per customer)
   - Collaborations with popular Solidity development communities and organizations
   - Sponsorship of Solidity-focused events and meetups
